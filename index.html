Xun Lin (Simplified Chinese character: 林汛，Cantonese: Lam Seon)

I am a fourth year phd student at Tsinghua University (YMSC), Beijing, China(PRC). My supervisor is Professor William Donovan.

My research is in algebraic geometry. I am interested in the geometry of derived categories, especially the indecomposability problems. I am also interested in Hochschild (co)homology theories, para-canonical systems, generic vanishing theorems and birational geometry, and the rationality problems of cubic fourfolds.
